二路 Èrlù

Lit. second road, it is the second of two form routines in Chen-style Taijiquan. It is also called 炮捶 pào chuí or cannon fist.

Practical Method#

In relation to the 一路 Yīlù#, the analogy is: the yilu lays the tracks, and the erlu puts the train on the tracks.
